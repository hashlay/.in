Got Questions?

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything you need to know about Hashlay products, delivery, and care.

How do I use the Hashlay Sneaker Foam? ▼
Apply a small amount of foam directly onto the cleaning brush. Scrub gently in circular motions on the sneaker surface. Wipe clean with the included microfiber cloth. Allow to air dry naturally away from direct sunlight. For heavy stains, repeat the process once more.
Is Hashlay Foam safe for suede sneakers? ▼
Yes! Our pH-balanced formula is specifically tested and safe for suede, nubuck, leather, mesh, canvas, and synthetic materials. We recommend using lighter pressure on delicate suede surfaces and always use the soft-bristle brush to avoid any material damage.
How long does delivery take? ▼
Standard delivery takes 3–5 business days across India. Metro cities (Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Chennai, Hyderabad) typically receive orders within 2–3 days. Northeast and remote areas may take up to 7 business days. You'll receive an SMS/WhatsApp update once your order ships.
Is Cash on Delivery (COD) available? ▼
Yes, Cash on Delivery is available across most pin codes in India. A small COD handling charge is applied at checkout. Some remote areas may not support COD — online payment is always available as an alternative for all locations.
What is the return/refund policy? ▼
We offer a 7-day hassle-free return policy. If you're not satisfied with your product, contact us at contacthashlay@gmail.com within 7 days of delivery with photos of the product. Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days to your original payment method.
Does the foam provide waterproof protection? ▼
The Hashlay Sneaker Foam primarily cleans and restores your sneakers. It provides a light protective coating that repels minor water splashes. For heavy-duty waterproofing, we recommend using our foam as a base clean before applying a dedicated water repellent spray.
How do I use the Pro Cleaning Brush correctly? ▼
Use the brush in gentle circular motions with moderate pressure. For uppers and delicate materials, use light strokes. For soles and midsoles, you can apply more pressure to remove stubborn dirt. Always use the brush with foam — never dry scrub, as this may cause light scratching on sensitive materials.
How often should I clean my sneakers? ▼
For daily wear sneakers, we recommend a light clean after every 3–4 wears and a thorough clean once every 2 weeks. For special occasion sneakers or limited editions, clean after every wear. Regular cleaning prevents dirt buildup and extends the life of your sneakers significantly.
